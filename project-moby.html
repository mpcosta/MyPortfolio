MobyDick-Tours

MobyDick-Tours is a local whale watching company in which I had the pleasure to work as a Systems Analyst and Web Developer. My main objective was rising their presence on the web which was really low at the time. I collected information from their targeted clients and analysed it in order to improve their visibality on the web and apply the correct SEO (Search Engine Optimization) techniques. I enjoyed bringing this local company to the web in order to improve and grow their business.
