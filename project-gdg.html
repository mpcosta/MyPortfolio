GDG Azores

Google Developer Group Azores is a group created to motivate and help the local developer community to take the ideas they have and turn them into reality. Since its creation, we have helped hundreds of people by either teaching them about a new technology or by connecting them with companies. We do all this by creating several events during each year in order to get the community excited about technology.

It is a way for helping people think beyond and be able to create their ideas along with the objective of showing that "Everything Is Connected".
