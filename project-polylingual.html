Polylingual

Polylingual is an E-learning web platform that allows the connection between language teachers and students who are taking their classes. This platform enables the teacher user to choose from a variety of different types of exercises and customize them for their respective class. On the other side, the student can then solve these exercises and get feedback immediately as soon as they complete them.

The platform also integrates some extra features like the possibility of the students talk to each other through forums, the possibility of them hearing the question out loud and the possibility of them training the language by chatting with an AI Bot.
