Infinitum

Infinitum is a platform under development based entirely on AR (Augmented Reality). This platform is going to launch several projects for different industries such as the tourism and real estate sector.

Infinitum allows the user to access and interact with real time information displayed on his screen while he points the camera of his mobile device. This data can then be collected for deep analysis of how the flow of people is around certain locations and with it improve the world around us.
