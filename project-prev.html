Prevention

Prevention: the best medicine is a game developed for an external client about preventive medicine that will provide you with knowledge that, if applied to your daily choices, will make you live more actively, healthier and feeling better. It includes the knowledge of the European Guidelines on cardiovascular disease prevention in clinical practice.

This game was developed using Unity Engine 4.0, and it had an educational purpose of teaching the users to about healthy habits and apply them in their daily lifes.
