App Azores

App Azores is an initiative of creating a Brand to represent all, current and future, personal and group projects. It has started on my last year of high school where my partner and I created four games on the same year. Its end purpose is the creation of a company to account all creative and practical projects that can be released for both the enterprise and general community audience.

This brand is growing by each year and it will eventually represent more sophisticated and innovative projects along with a total renewal of the App Azores Logo and Web Channels.
